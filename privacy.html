Legal · Privacy

Privacy, stated plainly.

This website is built to practice the digital sovereignty it describes: no cookies, no analytics, no trackers, no advertising. This statement explains the little that remains.

Last updated: 8 July 2026

1. Who is responsible

This website is operated by Non-Extractive Capital (“NEC”), Science Park 608, 1098 XH Amsterdam, the Netherlands, reachable at support@nonextractivecapital.com. NEC’s Dutch legal entities are in incorporation; until incorporation completes, NEC’s founding team operates this website and is responsible for the limited processing described below. This statement will be updated to name the operating entity once it is incorporated.

2. What this website does not do

This website sets no cookies. It runs no analytics or audience measurement, uses no tracking pixels, fingerprinting, or advertising technology, embeds no social-media widgets, and offers no accounts or forms. We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data — with anyone, for anything. A website about sovereign infrastructure should not itself be an instrument of surveillance.

3. Server logs

Like any website, ours is delivered by a hosting provider whose servers process technical access data — your IP address, the page requested, the time of the request, and your browser type — in server logs, as technically necessary to deliver the site and keep it secure. The legal basis is our legitimate interest in operating a secure website (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR). Log data is not combined with other data, is not used to identify visitors, and is retained no longer than the provider’s standard log rotation. This site is currently served by GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc.); GitHub may process this technical access data on servers outside the EEA. The hosting arrangement will be reconfirmed here when the production deployment is finalized.

4. Fonts

The site’s two typefaces (Cormorant Garamond and Inter) are self-hosted: the font files are delivered from this website’s own server together with the rest of the site. Loading them causes no request to any third party — no font service, no CDN — and accordingly no transmission of your IP address to anyone but the hosting provider described in section 3.

5. Email

If you write to support@nonextractivecapital.com, we process your email address and the contents of your message in order to handle the correspondence (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR, or Article 6(1)(b) where your message precedes an agreement). Correspondence is kept as long as handling it requires and is then deleted. Your address is not added to any mailing list — we do not operate one.

6. External links and tools

This website links to external services — for example, the DSF portfolio simulator, downloadable documents, and the websites of partner organizations. When you follow such a link, the operator of that service processes your data under its own privacy terms; this statement covers only this website.

7. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access the personal data we hold about you, and to rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and objection to processing based on legitimate interest. Since we process almost nothing, exercising these rights will usually be short work — write to support@nonextractivecapital.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Dutch supervisory authority, the Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens.

8. No automated decision-making

We carry out no profiling and no automated decision-making of any kind through this website.

9. Changes

When this statement changes, the updated version is published here with a new date. Material changes — a new processor, a new purpose — will be stated, not slipped in.
