Not an offer — stated formally.

Last updated: 8 July 2026

1. No offer, no solicitation

Nothing on this website, and nothing in any document downloadable from it, constitutes or forms part of an offer, invitation, solicitation, or recommendation to subscribe for, buy, or otherwise participate in any fund, security, or financial instrument, in any jurisdiction. No subscription or commitment can be made through this website, and none will be accepted.

2. The fund is in development

Digital Sovereignty Fund Coöperatie U.A. is being established and is not open for investment. The structures, caps, policies, and governance described on this website reflect working documents that are under professional review and may change before any launch. Where this website describes how the fund is designed to work, it describes intent and architecture — not an existing offer and not final terms.

3. Professional investors only

If and when an offer is made, it will be addressed exclusively to professional investors within the meaning of Article 1:1 of the Dutch Financial Supervision Act (Wft), and it will be made solely by means of the fund’s own offering documentation — never through this website. No offer will be made to retail investors. The applicable regime provides no “opt-up”: a party that does not meet the statutory definition of a professional investor cannot participate, whatever the intended amount and however sophisticated the party.

4. Regulatory status

NEC Fund Management B.V. intends to register with the AFM under the registration regime of Article 2:66a Wft; the registration is in preparation and has not been completed, and no marketing of the fund takes place before it is. Managers registered under that regime are not subject to ongoing prudential supervision by the AFM or De Nederlandsche Bank, and registration, once completed, is not an endorsement by any authority. The protections the fund offers investors arise from its own documents and structures — which is why they are published.

5. Models, papers, and the simulator

The working papers, the model figures shown on this website, and the interactive simulator are research tools. They show the mechanics of a model under stated assumptions, so the design can be inspected, stress-tested, and challenged. They are illustrative only: nothing in them is a projection, forecast, or promise of the performance of any fund or investment, and no figure in them may be relied upon as such.

6. Risk

An investment of the kind described here would place capital genuinely at risk. The contemplated instruments rank behind creditors, carry no guarantees and no security; returns are capped while losses are not; interests are illiquid, with a lock-up of no less than five years and no public market; and the total loss of any investment is possible. This summary does not replace the risk disclosures of any future offering documentation, which alone would govern.

Nothing on this website is investment, legal, or tax advice, and nothing on it takes any person’s individual circumstances into account. Anyone considering the themes described here should take their own professional advice.

Statements on this website about intentions and plans — incorporation, registrations, pipelines, publication of documents — reflect NEC’s current intent in a project under active development. They are updated as the work progresses, but they are not commitments, and they should not be relied upon as predictions.
